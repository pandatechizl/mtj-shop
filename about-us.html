About Us

Welcome to Magnificent Treasure Jewellery Pte Ltd — a trusted name in fine gemstones and exquisite jewellery. Based in Singapore, we specialise in authentic and ethically sourced precious stones including emerald, jade, moissanite, sapphire, ruby, and more.

Our mission is simple — to bring you timeless elegance, genuine craftsmanship, and lasting value. Each piece in our collection is meticulously curated and certified to ensure superior quality and authenticity.

As a registered PSPM dealer, we uphold the highest ethical standards and transparent business practices. Whether you’re seeking a meaningful gift or a personal statement piece, our jewellery celebrates beauty, confidence, and grace.

About the Brand

At Magnificent Treasure Jewellery (MTJ), we believe that true beauty lies in timeless elegance. Established in Singapore, our brand is dedicated to offering authentic, high-quality gemstones and fine jewellery that embody sophistication and enduring value.

Each creation is carefully curated to highlight the natural brilliance of gemstones — from exquisite emeralds to sparkling moissanite and radiant jade. Our artisans ensure that every piece reflects both artistry and integrity.

As a registered dealer under the PSPM Act, MTJ is committed to ethical sourcing, transparency, and authenticity. Every gemstone is accompanied by trusted certification and quality assurance.

Whether for daily elegance or a lifetime occasion, MTJ helps you shine with confidence, grace, and a touch of magnificence.
